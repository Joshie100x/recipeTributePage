How to Cook

Follow These Instructions For A Great Pie That the Entire Family Will Enjoy

Filling

  • 5 peaches, sliced
  • 2 Tbsp Truvia Cane Sugar Blend
  • 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ½ Tbsp cornstarch

Topping

  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ½ cup Truvia Cane Sugar Blend
  • ½ cup flour
  • ¾ cup oats
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Vanilla Whipped Cream

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 Tbsp Truvia Cane Sugar Blend
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract or vanilla beans

Directions

  1. Stir filling ingredients together in a large mixing bowl and transfer to an 8" x 8" baking dish.
  2. Mix topping ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Crumble the topping mixture on top of peaches in the baking dish.
  4. Bake at 350° F for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Beat whip cream until soft peaks form. Add Truvia Cane Sugar Blend and vanilla.
  6. Top the peach crisp with the whipping cream and serve.
